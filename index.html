Our Services

Mobile Disease Detection

Instant crop disease diagnosis using smartphone cameras with AI-powered image recognition for maize, tomatoes, and beans.

Offline Diagnosis

TensorFlow Lite integration enables disease detection even without internet connectivity, perfect for rural farming areas.

Analytics & Insights

Comprehensive analytics dashboard tracking disease patterns, treatment effectiveness, and crop health trends.

Multi-language Support

User-friendly interface available in English and Swahili, designed specifically for Kenyan farmers.

Real-time API

RESTful API backend providing fast, accurate disease predictions with cloud storage and analytics.

Treatment Recommendations

Actionable treatment advice and prevention strategies tailored to specific diseases and local conditions.
